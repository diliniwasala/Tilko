Hotels

Hotels usually have at least 50 rooms, but larger hotels can have up to 500 rooms and suites. Hotels can be found to suite any budget in Sri Lanka, TILKO Travels and Tours has its own brand TILKO City hotel Jaffna and are ideal for families or large groups because of their many facilities and activities. Sri Lanka has quite a few Colonial style hotels that are usually Colonial Mansions transformed into luxurious hotels, yet preserving their former grandeur and character.

Resorts

A resort is a recreation complex that normally has a center of attraction, like a beach, a garden park, a swimming pool and a restaurant.

Boutique

Boutique Hotels are a recent trend in Sri Lanka. Usually with just 3-15 rooms, they offer a quietness and privacy that is hard to find in larger hotels. Every Butique Hotel has its own unique style and its smaller size means more personalize service. Ideal for an intimate get-away with someone special, Boutique Hotels are known for their atmosphere of tranquility and romance. More expensive than your average hotel, boutique hotels have a magical charm not easily forgetten.

Bangalows

Bungalows are small homes that were once Colonial properties but have since been renovated for modern use. Bungalows in the hill country were originally build as holiday cottages for British tea-planters, and many been re-furbished to be luxuarious and comfortable. Bungalows most often have their own kitchen and some even come complete with maid and cook. Although don’t have all the facilities of a large hotel, they are a cost –effective alternative for larger groups.

Apartment

Apartment are service houses for renting short stay or longer stay, a unit comes with more than a bed room, May be 2 to 3 bedrooms in single unit, the apartments are equipped with gymnasium, swimming pool, groceries and kiddy play area.
