Who Are We

Nowhere else on earth can you watch the whales at day break, roam with the elephants in the evening, witness the sun rise over the mountains on the following day, and be back in time to catch a fabulous sunset over a golden-brown horizon.

Here in the paradise isle, we want you to have a great experience while you’re in Sri Lanka. Whether you’re a solo traveller venturing out into the wild or you are here on holiday with friends and family, we want your to enjoy everything that our tiny island has to offer.

We assure that all aspects of your tour are looked into so you don’t have to.

At Tilko Travels & Tours, we cultivate a culture of sustainable leisure in how the visitor enjoys their stay here without leaving nothing but their footsteps and memories behind. Tilko Travels and Tours is a subsidiary of a UK based business conglomerate with entrepreneurial interests and offices in London, Colombo, Trincomalee and Jaffna. Our experience in the travel trade spans across 30 years and caters to diverse corporate and private customer base.

So, join us. Witness the wonders of the cultures and communities we have here. The people who are part of the diversity that is Sri Lanka. We welcome you to share our inheritance; the beauty and the culture that has moulded our nation. We invite you to embrace this diversity that makes us who we are.

Ayubowan! Vanakkam! Welcome!

In the island like no other.
